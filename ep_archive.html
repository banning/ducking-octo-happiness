Episode Archive:

Episode I: Getting the Apartment
The major characters are introduced as Simon, Guy, and Frank explore an inheritance that will finally let them live on their own. New friends are made, and strange forces are unleashed.

Episode II: Baseball
America's favorite past time and free novelty items draw the guys out of their humble apartment...while the Evil Face's demands of Fro grow more sinister.

Episode III: Camping
Realizing graduation means they may part ways forever, the gang goes on an outing to celebrate their successes and remember the good days. Within lies a horrifying glimpse of the future...

Episode IV: Coffee Shop
Faced with the utter meaninglessness of existence, Guy takes a job with the Walden police department to instill a sense of purpose in his life. What will his radical, left-wing art friends think of him now?

Episode V: Crop Circles
A farmland prank brings new age mystics, tourists, and curiosity seekers to Walden, and the townies use the opportunity for profit and love.
