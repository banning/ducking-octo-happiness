Supporting cast biographies:
Rich Cory lived down the hall from room 323 until one calm summer night...
The Mail Man is a loving father working hard to put his daughter through college, but tends to have trouble controlling his anger.

Coffee Shop Manager runs the Javacrucian Order, which took over the former McKenna Greenhouse. In addition to brewing caffeinated beverages, the manager is a key player in Walden's illicit drug trade.

Tim is president of the theatre club and leader of the radical left-wing militant group on Walden campus. His major concerns are putting on productions, bringing down the Campus Republicans, and mocking the school's anti-smoking rules.
George was once a mild-mannered art student paying his way through school by working at the analytical cube factory until one fateful day when there was a horrific explosion...
Professor Robbins was a distinguished researcher at Walden University who had earned the respect and admiration of his colleagues and students during his long and prestigious career dedicated to aiding humanity and furthering the pursuit of knowledge...until a neoplasmic conspiracy among the highest levels of power blackened his reputation and turned his friends and family against him. He can now be found sitting in his corner at the Javacrucian Order, petting his favorite plants and giving sage wisdom to the youth of Walden.
