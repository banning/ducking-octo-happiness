____ Ignoring the cartoonishly absurd appearance of the parrots, the actions here are actually atrosciously horrendous instead of just comically amusing. Many people tend to get parrots as pets thinking they're simple animals to keep and soon realize they're highly intelligent social creatures requiring constant attention. When they don't get the care they need, they tend to become mentally ill, self mutilating and developing anti-social behavior (of course chickens show no where near the same cognitive levels as parrots and will still peck themselves to death in tiny cages, hence their debeaking by corporate farmers. I'm lovin' it!). While their speech tends to be viewed as just mimicking, African Greys (like the one caricatured here) have shown the ability to combine words they already know to name novel objects (such as green key) and to count and comprehend the concept of zero (take that Mayans). So Ozzy might as well be killing dolphins or two-year-old humans here. So just in case you didn't think this was as bad as auto-erotic asphyxiation or suicide, now you know.
